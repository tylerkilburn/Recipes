Coconut Cookies

Directions

Preheat oven to 350˚. Combine the flour, baking soda, and salt; set aside.

In a medium bowl, cream the butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until smooth. Beat in the egg and vanilla until light and fluffy. Gradually blend in the flour mixture, then mix in the coconut. Drop dough by teaspoonfuls onto an ungreased cookie sheet. Cookies should be about 3 inches apart.

Bake for 11 minutes in 350˚ preheated oven, or until lightly toasted. Cool on wire racks.

Notes

Alterations:
Substituted 8 TBL Margarine for 8 TBL Butter
Substituted 1/2 tsp Baking Powder for 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
