Homemade Vanilla Wafers

Cookies

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Servings: Servings: 65 Source: bakingamoment.com

Ingredients

1 1/4 cup AP Flour

156g

1/2 tsp Salt

————————

1/2 cup Butter (softened)

1/2 cup Powdered Sugar

1/4 cup Sugar

————————

1/4 cup Milk

1 1/2 tsp Vanilla

Directions

1 Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F and line baking sheets with parchment paper.

2 Place the butter, powdered sugar, and granulated sugar in a large mixing bowl, and beat on medium speed until pale and fluffy (3 to 5 minutes).

3 Scrape the bottom and sides of the bowl with a silicone spatula, then mix in the milk and vanilla until combined.

4 Stir in the flour and salt, taking care not to overwork the dough.

5 Transfer the dough to a pastry bag fitted with a 1A tip. Pipe 1-inch diameter circles onto the prepared baking sheets.

6 Bake for 20 to 24 minutes, or until golden brown on the bottoms and around the edges.

7 Cool completely, then store in an airtight container.
