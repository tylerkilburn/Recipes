Roasted Butternut Squash with Root Vegetables

Meals

Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 40 minutes Servings: Servings 4 Source: dinneratthezoo.com

Ingredients

2 cups of Butternut Squash

2 cups of Carrots

2 cups of Sweet Potatoes

————————

2 TBL Olive Oil

2 TBL Honey

1/2 tsp Kosher Salt

1/2 tsp Black Pepper

Directions

1 Preheat your oven to 400 degrees. Line a sheet pan with foil coated in cooking spray.

2 Mix together the olive oil and honey in a small bowl.

3 Spread the vegetables out on the sheet pan in a single layer.

4 Drizzle the olive oil mixture over the top, use your hands to toss the vegetables around to evenly coat. Season generously with salt and pepper.

5 Roast for 40 minutes or until vegetables are tender and starting to caramelize. If desired, sprinkle with sage leaves and coarse salt before serving (optional).

Notes

Make Ahead: I always prep the vegetables at least a day in advance.
