Tomato Soup

Meals

Prep Time: 5 minutes Cook Time: 15 minutes Total Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

56 oz Crushed Tomatoes (28oz / can)

6 oz Tomato Paste ( 6oz / can )

2 cup Water

2 TBL Heavy Cream

2 TBL Margarine

1/4 cup Dried Basil

3 tsp Garlic Powder

1/4 tsp Pepper

1/2 tsp Salt

Fresh Basil to taste

Directions

1. Heat crushed tomatoes and tomato paste on low.

2. Add in remaining ingredients and simmer for 20 minutes.
