Vegetable Tikka Masala

Meals

Prep Time: 25 min Cook Time: 15 min Total Time: 40 min Servings: Yield: 4 servings Source: aimeemars.com

Ingredients

Seasoning:

1/2 tsp Salt

2 tsp Ginger

1/2 tsp Ground Cloves

1 tsp Coriander

1 tsp Cumin

1 tsp Garam Masala

1/2 tsp Cardamom

2 tsp Tumeric

3 tsp Garlic (minced)


Sauce:

1 TBL Butter

————————

2 Potatoes (chopped)

1 Carrot (chopped)

————————

2 Onions (chopped)

1 Bell Pepper (chopped)

————————

8 oz Mushrooms (sliced)

15 oz Garbanzo Beans

————————

2 TBL Red Chili Paste

1/2 cup Crushed Tomatoes

14 oz Coconut Milk


Fresh Cilantro for Garnish

Directions

1 Seasoning In a small bowl combined the Ginger, minced Garlic, Ground Cloves, Tumeric, Garam Masala, Coriander, Cumin, and Cardamom until well mixed. Set aside.

2 Drain garbanzo beans and chop vegetables.

3 Prepare the Sauce
- Melt the Butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
- Add Potatoes and Carrots, cook for 7 minutes.
- Stir, cook for additional 7 minutes.
- Add the Onions and Bell Pepper, cook stirring occasionally for about 3 minutes.
- Add the Mushrooms and Garbanzo Beans and continue cooking for an additional 5 minutes.
- Add Spice mixture.
- Add Crushed Tomatoes and Coconut Milk.

4 Turn the heat down to a simmer and let cook for about 15 minutes more when the sauce will begin to thicken slightly.
