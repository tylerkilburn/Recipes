Buttermilk Chocolate Bread

Breads (Quick)

Prep Time: 10 min Cook Time: 1hr 5mins Servings: Serves: 12 Source: geniuskitchen.com

Ingredients

1 3/4 AP Flour

219 g

1/2 cup Cocoa Powder

1/2 tsp Salt

1/2 tsp Baking Powder

1/2 tsp Baking Soda

1/2 tsp Espresso Powder

————————

8 TBL Butter (softened)

1 cup Sugar

2 Eggs

1 cup Whole Buttermilk

1 tsp Vanilla

————————

1/3 cup Chocolate Chips (optional)


Topping:

Chocolate Glaze (optional)

Description

Mix Method: Cream
Use Cake Release
Use Parchment Paper

Directions

Heat oven to 350*.

Grease bottom only of 8x4 or 9x5-inch loaf pan.

In a large bowl, combine sugar and margarine; blend well.

Add eggs; blend well.

Stir in buttermilk.

Add flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt; stir just until dry ingredients are moistened.

Stir in nuts.

Pour in pan coated with cake release and lined with parchment paper.

Bake at 350* for 55 to 65 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.

Cool 15 minutes; remove from pan.

Cool completely.

Wrap tightly and store in refrigerator.

Yield: 1 loaf (12 slice).

Notes

Alterations:
Added 1/3 cup chocolate chips (optional)
