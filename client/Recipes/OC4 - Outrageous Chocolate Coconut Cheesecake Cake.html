Directions

Session 1:

1 Do prep work in part 1 to make the rest of the process go smooth.

2 Make the Pecan Filing in part 2 and cool.

3 Make the layers in parts 3 - 5.


Session 2:

4 Place the cooled and firm brownie cheesecake onto a serving plate and top with another 1/3 of the coconut pecan filling and spread into an even layer.

5 Add the chocolate cake layer to the top of the cake.

6 Frost the sides of the cake with chocolate frosting, then press mini chocolate chips into the frosting.

7 Pipe swirls of the remaining frosting around the top edge of the cake and add the remaining coconut pecan filling to the top/center of the cake.

8 Refrigerate cake until ready to serve. Cake is best for 4-5 days.
