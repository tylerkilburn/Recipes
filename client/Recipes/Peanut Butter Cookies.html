Peanut Butter Cookies

_Refining, Cookies

Prep Time: 15 m Cook Time: 10 m Servings: 12 servings Source: allrecipes.com

Ingredients

8 TBL Margarine

1/2 cup Peanut Butter

1/2 cup Brown Sugar

1/2 cup Sugar

1 Egg

—————————

1 1/4 cup AP Flour (semi packed)

165 g

1/4 tsp Salt

1/2 tsp Baking Powder

3/4 tsp Baking Soda

Description

Mix Method: Cream
Oven: 375

Directions

1 Cream butter, peanut butter, and sugars together in a bowl; beat in eggs.

2 In a separate bowl, sift flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt; stir into butter mixture. Put dough in refrigerator for 1 hour.

3 Roll dough into 1 inch balls and put on baking sheets. Flatten each ball with a fork, making a crisscross pattern. Bake in a preheated 375˚ oven for about 10 minutes or until cookies begin to brown.

Notes

Alterations:
Substituted 8 TBL Margarine for 8 TBL Butter
Increased Flour to 165g from 156g
