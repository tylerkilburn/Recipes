White Bread

Breads (Yeast)

Prep Time: 2 hours Cook Time: 30 minutes Total Time: 2 1/2 Hours Servings: 1 Loaf

Ingredients

2 1/2 tsp Yeast

1 TBL Sugar

1 cup 110 deg Water

—————————

3 cup Bread Flour

360g

1/4 Ginger

1 tsp Salt

—————————

1 TBL Margarine (warm)

1 Egg

Description

Score Bread

Directions

1. Mix and Kneed.

2. 1st Rise: 45 minutes.

3. 2nd Rise: Put in pan for 45 minutes.

4. Bake at 350˚ for 30 minutes.
