Chocolate Chip Cookies

Cookies

Prep Time: 20 m Cook Time: 10 m Servings: 24 servings Source: allrecipes.com

Ingredients

1 1/2 cup AP Flour (slightly packed)

188 g

1/4 tsp Salt

1/2 tsp Baking Soda

————————

8 TBL Margarine

1/2 cup Brown Sugar

1/2 cup Sugar

————————

1 tsp Vanilla

1 Egg

————————

1 cup Chocolate Chips

1/2 cup Walnuts (optional)

Description

Mix Method: Cream
#50 Scoop
Margarine Adjusted - Extra 15g Flour

Directions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.

Bake for about 11-13 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.

#50: 11-13 minutes
#70: 9-12 minutes

Notes

Alterations:
Substituted 8 TBL Margarine for 8 TBL Butter
