Ingredients

Chocolate Cake Prep:

1/4 cup + 3 TBL AP Flour

53 g

1/2 cup Sugar

3 TBL Cocoa Powder

1/4 tsp Salt

1/8 tsp Baking Powder

1/2 tsp Baking Soda

1/4 tsp Espresso Powder

————————

1 Egg White


Brownie Layer Prep:

3/4 cup AP Flour

90 g

1 cup Sugar

6 TBL Baking Cocoa

1/4 tsp Salt

1/4 tsp Baking Powder

1/2 tsp Espresso Powder

————————

10 TBL Butter (cut)


Cheesecake: Layer Prep:

2/3 cup Sugar

2 TBL AP Flour

————————

1/2 cup Chocolate Chips

————————

2/3 cup Sour Cream

————————

16 oz Cream Cheese (room temp)


Pecan Filing Prep:

2 2/3 cup Shredded Coconut

1 1/4 cup Pecans (chopped)

12 TBL Butter (cut)

————————

6 Egg Yolk

Directions

1 Make sure kitchen is clean, free of dishes and sink is empty and ready for the long prep.

2 Have at least two TBL of cake release on hand.

3 Cut out 2 parchment paper disks.

Chocolate Cake Layer Prep:

4 Combine dry ingredients in 3qt bowl and whisk.

5 Place 1 egg white in 1 1/2 cup sized measuring pourer, wisk. Place left over yolk in 2qt sauce pan.

Brownie Layer Prep:

6 Combine dry ingredients in 4qt bowl and whisk.

7 Measure 10 TBL butter; cut and put on plate.

Cheesecake: Layer Prep:

8 Measure out sugar, flour and set aside.

9 Measure out sour cream and set aside.

10 Measure out chocolate chips and set aside.

11 Cut cream cheese, place in large 5qt bowl and set aside with heavy duty silicone spatula.


Pecan Filing Prep:

12 Preheat oven to 350˚.

13 Ready 5 more egg yolks in saucepan for total of 6. Save remaining whites in container for something else.

14 Chop 1 1/4 cups pecans.

15 Make Toasted Pecans by baking Pecans on sprayed baking sheet at 350˚ for 6 minutes.

16 Measure out 2 2/3 cup Shredded Coconut and set aside.

17 Measure 12 TBL butter; cut and put on plate.

Continue onto step two when pecans come out
