Apple Pie

Pies

Cook Time: prep: 20 min. bake: 45 min. Servings: 8 servings Source: tasteofhome.com

Ingredients

1 Pie Crust

————————

1/2 cup Sugar

1/2 cup Brown Sugar

3 TBL AP Flour

1 TBL Corn Starch

2 tsp Cinnamon

1/4 tsp Nutmeg

1/4 tsp Ginger

————————

4-5 cups Tart Apples (peeled, thinly sliced, chopped)

1 TBL Lemon Juice

————————

1 TBL butter

Directions

1 In a small bowl, combine the sugars, flour and spices; set aside. In a large bowl, toss apples with lemon juice. Add sugar mixture; toss to coat.

2 Line a 9-in. pie plate with bottom crust; trim even with edge. Fill with apple mixture; dot with butter. Roll remaining crust to fit top of pie; place over filling. Trim, seal and flute edges. Cut slits in crust.

3 Beat egg white until foamy; brush over crust. Sprinkle with sugar. Cover edges loosely with foil.

4 Bake at 375° for 50 minutes. Remove foil after the first 25 minutes. Cool on a wire rack.

Notes

Alterations:
Increased Ground Cinnamon to 2 tsp from 1 tsp
Added 1 TBL Corn Starch

Nutrition

1 piece: 414 calories, 16g fat (7g saturated fat), 14mg cholesterol, 227mg sodium, 67g carbohydrate (38g sugars, 2g fiber), 3g protein.
