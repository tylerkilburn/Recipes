Eggnog Cookies

Prep Time: 20 min Cook Time: 23 min Servings: Yield: 3 dozen cookies Source: recipegirl.com

Ingredients

2 1/4 cup AP Flour

281g

1/8 tsp Salt

1 tsp Baking Powder

1/2 tsp Cinnamon

1 tsp Nutmeg

————————

3/4 cup Butter (softened)

1 1/4 cup Sugar

————————

1/4 cup Whole Milk

2 TBL Heavy Cream

1 tsp Vanilla

1 Egg

2 Egg Yolk

Description

Mix Method: Cream
Lightly Sprinkle with Nutmeg

Directions

1. Preheat oven to 300°F.

2. In a small bowl, combine all the dry ingredients together. In a large bowl, cream together sugar and butter. Add remaining ingredients and beat at medium speed until smooth. Add dry ingredients and beat at low speed just until blended.

3. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheet, 1 inch apart. Sprinkle lightly with additional nutmeg.

4. Bake for 23-25 minutes or until bottoms turn light brown. Transfer to wire racks to cool.

Notes

Alterations:
Substituted 1/4 cup Whole Milk for 1/2 cup Egg Nog
Added 2 TBL Heavy Cream
Increased nutmeg to 1 tsp from 1/2 tsp
Added 1 Egg
