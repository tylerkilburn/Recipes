Raspberry Pie

Pies

Prep Time: 30 m Cook Time: 40 m Servings: 8 servings Source: allrecipes.com

Ingredients

1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie

————————

4 cup Raspberries

1 cup Sugar

2 1/2 TBL Cornstarch

1 TBL Lemon Juice

1/4 tsp Cinnamon

1/8 tsp Salt

————————

1 TBL Butter


Directions

1 Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).

2 Mix together the raspberries, sugar, tapioca, lemon juice, cinnamon, and salt until raspberries are well-covered.

3 Pour into 9 or 10-inch pastry shell. Dot with butter; add top crust and crimp edge.

4 (Optional) Make slits in the top crust and brush with cream.

5 Foil foil on to edges to shield from burning.

6 Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes to set the crust.

7 Reduce heat to 375˚ and 25 minutes more.

Notes

Alterations:
Substituted 2 1/2 Cornstarch for 2 1/2 Tapioca
