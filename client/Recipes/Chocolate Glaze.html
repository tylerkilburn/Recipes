Chocolate Glaze

Glazes

Source: epicurious.com

Ingredients

3/4 cup Powdered Sugar

2 TBL Cocoa Powder

1 tsp Vanilla

1 TBL Milk

Directions

1. Sift together the sugar and cocoa powder in a medium bowl. Slowly stir in the milk and vanilla, a little at a time, to make a smooth, pourable glaze.
